Spiritual Companions - Praying


The Spiritual Companions program aims at helping you deepen your personal faith, your relationships with Jesus and others in the Kingdom of God. It provides an opportunity for you to pray with another person who also wants to grow in their faith.

One of the goals of this program is to help you make part of your daily life the following disciplines:

  • Daily personal private prayer
  • Participate in the Sacraments of the church regularly, especially Eucharist and Reconciliation
  • Begin to try to understand what is means to have the Holy Spirit residing inside of you
  • Become part of the faith-based community where you and your Spiritual Companion attend  Sunday Mass
  • Start serving in certain aspect of this faith-based community
  • Be spiritually nourish by partaking in the church’s Sacramentals (for example, praying the rosary)
