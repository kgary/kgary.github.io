Welcome to the Newman Club!

Named in honor of John Henry Cardinal Newman, Newman Centers and Clubs are Catholic ministry centers around the world that provide pastoral support to their Catholic communities. The John Paul 2 Catholic Newman Center is an outreach of the Diocese of Phoenix. In the Fall of 2006, Bishop Thomas J. Olmsted, seeking to serve Catholic students at ASU Polytechnic campus, appointed a chaplain to serve at the Poly campus. The name, "John Paul 2" was chosen for the Newman Center, because of the late pope's love for university students and his rich and academic teachings.
